If you're new to the concept of destination weddings, here are a few tips to get you started:

When selecting a wedding destination, consider both accessibility and your guest list. An offbeat location might be alluring, but it's crucial to ensure it is accessible for seniors or guests with disabilities. Evaluate whether your guests can afford the travel and accommodation costs, and check if the weather will be favorable for your event. Think about the type of backdrop you envision for your ceremony. If a beachside wedding has always been your dream, locations like Goa or Kerala offer beautiful coastal settings. For a grand, traditional ambiance, Rajasthan’s palatial venues provide the perfect setting.

Choosing The Right Date:

October to March is generally considered the best time for weddings, thanks to the pleasant weather and numerous auspicious dates available. However, your choice of date may also depend on your location. For example, hill stations like Mussoorie and Ooty can be quite cold during winter. If you’re aiming for more affordable destination wedding packages, consider scheduling your wedding during the off-season. With fewer crowds and reduced hotel bookings, costs are often lower, and professionals like makeup artists and photographers are more readily available.

At MC Tours & Travels, budget constraints are not an issue. We offer exceptional destination wedding packages, even during peak seasons. The shoulder season is also a fantastic option—it allows you to save money while enjoying favorable weather and a more relaxed atmosphere.

Best Destination Wedding Venues in India

We provide destination wedding packages for some of the most exotic locations in India. Here are a few of our top recommendations:

Namah, Jim Corbett

Namah Resort, located just 10 km from the picturesque Jim Corbett National Park, offers a unique wedding experience amidst wildlife and forested hills. Ideal for those seeking tranquility away from city life, the resort features over 3116 sq. ft of space, accommodating hundreds of guests. It boasts beautifully decorated banquet halls and a versatile ballroom that can be customized to meet your needs.

Wedding Cost: ₹20–25 lakhs


JaiBagh Palace, Jaipur

Set against the backdrop of the Aravalli Range, JaiBagh Palace in Jaipur is perfect for weddings and special occasions. The palace offers an indoor banquet hall, three outdoor lawns, and a poolside area, each capable of hosting hundreds of guests. Our destination wedding packages include comprehensive services such as food and beverage catering, decoration, lighting, photography, videography, and DJ services.

Wedding Cost: ₹25 lakhs


Royal Orchid Fort Resort, Mussoorie

Nestled in the serene valleys of Mussoorie, the Royal Orchid Fort Resort is a popular choice for destination weddings in India. The resort features two banquet halls and three open-air venues, all accommodating hundreds of guests. With 58 rooms available, your guests can enjoy a comfortable stay. Our packages cover catering, photography, videography, and decoration services.

Wedding Cost: ₹14 lakhs


Hotel Clarks Shiraz, Agra

Situated near the Taj Mahal, Hotel Clarks Shiraz is perfect for couples who envision their wedding with this iconic monument as a backdrop. Recognized by Conde Nast Traveller as one of the top hotels offering incredible views of the Taj Mahal, it is frequently acclaimed as one of North India’s best wedding destinations. The hotel features multiple wedding halls with luxurious interiors, which can be customized to your style. Additionally, its terrace is ideal for open-air events with stunning views of the city.

The Oberoi Udaivilas, Udaipur

The Oberoi Udaivilas is a renowned wedding venue favored by celebrities and the ultra-rich. Known for its opulence, it offers an unparalleled experience for those seeking a royal wedding. This venue epitomizes luxury and grandeur, making it the perfect choice for an extravagant celebration.

Wedding Cost: ₹1.2 crore

If you’re new to the idea of destination weddings, here are a few things to help you get started

Pick the Perfect Destination

When choosing a wedding destination, keep accessibility and the guest list in mind. An offbeat location is exciting, but is it accessible for seniors or relatives with a disability? Can your guests afford to pay for their travel and accommodation? Is the weather conducive for a wedding? What kind of backdrop would you love for the ceremony? If you’ve always dreamed about saying your I dos by the beach, consider a beach location in Goa or Kerala. Choose Rajasthan for its traditional and palatial ambiance if you love pomp and grandeur.

Choosing The Right Date:

October to March is the best season for weddings because the weather is pleasant and there are plenty of auspicious dates during these months. Your choice of date also depends on the location. For instance, hill stations like Mussoorie and Ooty are very cold during winter. If you’re looking for affordable destination wedding packages, choose a date in the off-season. Since there’s less crowd and fewer hotel bookings, wedding costs will be lower. Professionals like makeup artists and photographers are readily available.

With GT Holidays, budgets aren’t a problem. You can avail the best destination wedding packages, even during the peak season. Just let us know your chosen date, and we’ll reserve the venue, book your rooms, and engage professional artists like decorators and photographers immediately.

The shoulder season is a great time as well – you can save your guests and yourselves money and still experience the great weather.

Decide On The Budget

Destination weddings in India usually start from around INR 10 Lakh and could go up to INR 60 Lakhs or more, depending on the location, season, and number of guests. GT Holidays offers the best destination wedding packages irrespective of your budget.

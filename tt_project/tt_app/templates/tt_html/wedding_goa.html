Tips for a Destination Wedding in Goa

Planning a destination wedding in Goa? Here are some essential tips to ensure your special day is everything you dreamed of:

  • Visit the Venue: Make sure to visit the venue beforehand to ensure it meets your expectations and visualize how you want it to look for each event.
  • Meet Vendors Early: Schedule meetings with vendors well in advance to discuss all details and avoid last-minute misunderstandings.
  • Tailor Your Attire: Choose a wedding dress that suits the destination's climate and style.
  • Understand Local Requirements: Research local marriage requirements and ensure you have all necessary travel documents.

Tips for Planning a Destination Wedding in Sri Lanka

  • The ideal time for a destination wedding in Sri Lanka largely depends on the region where you plan to marry. For the southern and western coastal areas, the best period is from late November to April. During these months, you’ll enjoy exceptional temperatures, making it perfect for beach or tropical garden weddings. If you’re considering the eastern coastal regions, the pleasant weather from April to September provides a six-month window for dreamlike wedding celebrations. This period is ideal for those wanting to escape the extreme heat, dust, and rains of India during its off-season months.

  • Weddings in Sri Lanka are generally affordable, but costs can vary based on factors such as location, venue, duration, guest count, functions, entertainment options, and the services of local wedding planners and providers. For a more budget-friendly option, consider a small destination wedding package that focuses on a simple yet elegant celebration. Planning carefully can help you manage costs effectively while still creating a memorable event.

Top Wedding Venues in Sri Lanka

AVANI Resort Kalutara

The AVANI Kalutara Resort, nestled in a picturesque beach setting, is the perfect choice for a luxurious beach wedding. Located on a serene peninsula, this venue boasts vibrant gardens and a palm-fringed private beach. With its contemporary architecture and exceptional amenities, the resort offers an outdoor area that can host up to 200 guests and an elegant ballroom for up to 600 guests, making it one of the premier destination wedding venues in Sri Lanka.

Beruwala Cinnamon Bey

Located near Colombo’s bustling business district, Cinnamon Bey Beruwala provides a fantastic out-of-town option for large events. You can choose between an outdoor ceremony in their sea-view gardens, followed by a Bedouin-themed reception, or an open-air ceremony with a floating mandap adorned with your own decor. For indoor celebrations, the Kutlama Banquet Hall is versatile enough to accommodate both traditional and modern weddings or vow renewals.

Jetwing Blue

For a tranquil retreat away from city life, Jetwing Blue in Negombo is an excellent choice. This stylish beachside hotel offers a range of indoor and outdoor wedding venues, including a ballroom that accommodates 450 to 500 guests. If you’re dreaming of a beach wedding, their beachfront can host up to 800 guests, complete with a diverse selection of international cuisine and a well-stocked bar to enhance your celebration.

Best Beaches for a Destination Wedding in Sri Lanka

Best Resorts to Stay in Sri Lanka for Wedding Ceremonies

The Eden Resort & Spa

Situated on the golden shores of Beruwela, The Eden Resort & Spa is a luxurious 5-star retreat offering top-notch amenities and a memorable experience for every guest. Renowned as one of Sri Lanka’s premier spa resorts, it provides a range of indulgent therapies and beauty treatments—perfect for unwinding after a beautiful day at the beach. Its serene environment and exceptional services make it an ideal choice for a wedding stay.

The Fortress Resort & Spa

Reflecting a blend of colonial Dutch and Portuguese influences with Sri Lankan elements, The Fortress Resort & Spa on Sri Lanka’s southern coast transports guests back to the charm of Old Ceylon. Each room features private courtyards or balconies with breathtaking sea views, allowing you and your loved ones to start your day with stunning vistas while enjoying a delicious breakfast at one of the on-site restaurants. It’s a fantastic venue for a memorable wedding experience.

Amanwella Resort

Strategically located in Tangalle, a picturesque destination on Sri Lanka’s southern coast, Amanwella Resort offers a range of suites just steps from the beach, with terraces boasting exceptional ocean views. The resort ensures a memorable stay by offering various excursions, local recommendations, and engaging activities. It’s a perfect choice for couples seeking a blend of luxury and adventure during their wedding celebration.

From April to September, the east coast experiences its best weather, making it the ideal time to visit if you’re considering a wedding in that area.

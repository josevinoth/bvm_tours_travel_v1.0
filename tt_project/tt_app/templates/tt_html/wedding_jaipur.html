3. Tailor Your Wedding Outfit to the Destination: Consider the local climate when choosing your wedding attire. Jaipur's typically warm temperatures call for breathable fabrics. For winter weddings, layered and heavier materials can be a great choice.

Best Places for a Destination Wedding in Jaipur

Jaipur, with its array of opulent palaces and luxurious hotels, offers some of the most enchanting venues for a destination wedding. The city’s rich heritage and regal ambiance make it an ideal location for couples seeking a grand celebration. Below are some of the top resorts and venues in Jaipur for destination weddings, along with pricing details where available. Explore these exceptional locations to find the perfect backdrop for your special day.

Taj Jai Mahal Palace

Set amidst stunning Mughal gardens, the Taj Jai Mahal Palace, a 260-year-old heritage hotel, offers a truly romantic setting for weddings. This luxurious venue features a range of spaces, including the ceremony lawn, fountain lawn, poolside lawn, Durbar Hall, and palace lawn, each capable of accommodating between 100 to 3,000 guests.

Pride Amber Vilas Resort

For a regal experience, the Pride Amber Vilas Resort provides unmatched grandeur. The venue includes the central lawn, haveli, entrance lawn, and banquet halls, all designed to host up to 3,000 guests. Additionally, the resort offers striking rooms and catering services, both in-house and from third-party providers, ensuring a memorable celebration.

ITC Rajputana

For those drawn to luxury, ITC Rajputana is the perfect choice. This 5-star hotel exudes royalty and elegance, with the capacity to host up to 400 guests. It features in-house, multi-cuisine catering and world-class hospitality, ensuring your wedding is both sophisticated and spectacular.

Hotel Jaipur Darbar

Reflecting Rajput Mughal architecture with its elaborate corridors, frescoed walls, and exquisite audience halls, Hotel Jaipur Darbar offers an intimate setting for weddings. It can accommodate 75 to 100 guests, providing a charming and elegant venue for smaller celebrations.

Le Meridien

Le Meridien offers a prime location combined with exceptional services. The hotel features impressive venues such as the Diwan-E-Khas, perfect for engagements and pre-wedding ceremonies, and the Amer Ballroom, which is ideal for sangeet nights or receptions. These versatile spaces ensure a sophisticated and memorable wedding experience.

Hotel Narain Niwas Palace

Hotel Narain Niwas Palace, one of Jaipur’s exquisite heritage hotels, provides a magical setting for your grand day. Known for its enchanting ambiance and historical charm, this venue promises a wedding filled with lasting memories. The resort offers a delectable range of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes, adding to the grandeur of your celebration.

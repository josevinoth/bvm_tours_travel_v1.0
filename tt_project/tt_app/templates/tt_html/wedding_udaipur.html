Tips for best Udaipur Destination Wedding Packages

Destination weddings in Udaipur have become increasingly popular. Natural beauty, luxury hotels from major chains, and traditional hospitality make this a popular wedding destination.


If you’re planning a destination wedding in Udaipur, we’ll want to bring a few tips with you. And, here they are.

Plan ahead: –

Make sure to plan when it comes to Udaipur Destination Wedding Packages. Because it is one of the most popular wedding destinations, finding the wedding venue of your choice may take some time.

Notify your guests in advance: –

It’s important to give your guests enough time to figure out whether or not they can afford to travel to the destination. We suggest starting at least three months ahead of time, if not sooner.

Be aware of the weather: –

When deciding on a date for your destination wedding, the weather of the city is crucial. Because Udaipur experiences high temperatures from March to June, September to February is the best time to host a wedding there. These months are ideal for you and your guests to explore the wonders of the ‘City of Lakes.’

Make Use of Local Vendors: –

When you first arrive in Udaipur, ask the venue on the ground for vendor recommendations. Hiring local vendors is one of the best ways to save money when planning a destination wedding because they don’t have to travel or stay anywhere. In Udaipur, set up meetings with local florists and rental companies. Who knows, you might just discover a hidden gem in your backyard.

Adding a Rajasthani Touch: –

Whether you’re planning a wedding in a palace, a resort, or a 5-star hotel in Udaipur, you can always add a Rajasthani touch. Begin by deciding on royal-themed attire for the bride and groom. A sherwani with pajama or dhoti and a waistband or a Kamarbandh can be worn by the groom. The bride can wear a ghagra, choli, and odhani in a bright colour.

What is the cost of the Udaipur Destination Wedding package?

When planning a destination wedding in Udaipur, the number of guests, the number of days, and the number of functions are the most important factors to consider. In Udaipur, the average wedding budget is between 30 and 60 lakhs. For Udaipur Destination Wedding Packages with a guest list of about 150 people and a three-day celebration, you’ll need a minimum budget of around 30 – 60 lakhs. However, it is heavily influenced by the following factors:

  • Venue: – You must first decide whether you want to host the function and accommodate the guests in the same place. If it is not possible to accommodate guests at the function’s location, book a hotel near the location. There are both luxury hotels and budget hotels to choose from for a grand celebration or an intimate gathering.

  • The number of days: – The festivities usually last 3 to 4 days at a destination wedding. If you don’t want your wedding budget to balloon, it’s best to wrap up the festivities in two or three days.

  • List of Guests: – When planning a destination wedding in Udaipur, most people do not invite a large number of guests. Only close family and friends are invited to such a gathering, and the guest list is kept to a maximum of 150-200 people.

Of course, there is a slew of other factors that can influence your wedding budget, including photographers, makeup artists, entertainment, and transportation from the hotel to the venue.

Tips for Choosing the Best Udaipur Destination Wedding Packages

Udaipur has emerged as a highly sought-after destination for weddings, thanks to its breathtaking natural beauty, luxurious hotels from renowned chains, and exceptional traditional hospitality.

Planning a destination wedding in Udaipur? Here are some essential tips to help ensure everything goes smoothly:

Plan Ahead:
Given Udaipur’s status as a top wedding destination, it's crucial to start planning well in advance. Securing your preferred venue might take some time, so beginning early will help ensure you get the location you desire.

Notify Your Guests in Advance:
Provide your guests with ample notice to make travel arrangements. Ideally, inform them at least three months before the wedding to give them time to plan their trip and budget accordingly.

Be Aware of the Weather:
When choosing your wedding date, consider Udaipur’s weather. The city experiences high temperatures from March to June, so the best time to plan a wedding is between September and February. These cooler months will help you and your guests enjoy the city’s charm more comfortably.

Utilize Local Vendors:
Once you arrive in Udaipur, ask your venue for recommendations on local vendors. Hiring local suppliers can be a cost-effective option since they won’t need to travel or stay overnight. Meet with local florists and rental companies to find the best deals and potentially discover some hidden gems.

Add a Rajasthani Touch:
Whether your wedding is in a palace, resort, or five-star hotel, incorporate elements of Rajasthani culture into your celebration. Consider royal-themed attire: the groom might wear a sherwani with pajama or dhoti and a waistband or Kamarbandh, while the bride could choose a bright ghagra, choli, and odhani for an authentic touch.

What is the Cost of a Destination Wedding Package in Udaipur?

When planning a destination wedding in Udaipur, several factors influence the overall cost, including the number of guests, the duration of the celebration, and the number of events. On average, the budget for a wedding in Udaipur ranges between 30 and 60 lakhs. For a typical Udaipur Destination Wedding Package accommodating around 150 guests and spanning three days, you should expect to allocate a minimum budget of approximately 30 to 60 lakhs. However, costs can vary based on the following considerations:

  • Venue: Decide if you want the wedding and accommodation to be at the same location. If not, arrange a nearby hotel for your guests. Udaipur offers a range of options from luxurious hotels to more budget-friendly accommodations, suitable for both grand celebrations and intimate gatherings.
  • Number of Days: Destination weddings typically last 3 to 4 days. To keep your budget manageable, consider planning your celebrations within two or three days.
  • Guest List: Most destination weddings in Udaipur focus on close family and friends. Keeping the guest list to a maximum of 150-200 people can help control costs and create a more personal atmosphere.

Several other factors can impact your wedding budget, such as the costs of photographers, makeup artists, entertainment, and transportation between the hotel and the venue.

Tips for Planning a Destination Wedding in Kerala

When planning a destination wedding in Kerala, timing is crucial. Although Kerala enjoys pleasant weather year-round, the period between October and March is ideal for a romantic wedding. Here are some tips to ensure your wedding in Kerala is as perfect as you’ve imagined:

1. Choose the Right Season: The months from October to March offer the most favorable weather conditions, making it the best time for an outdoor wedding.
2. Venue Selection: Kerala is known for its diverse landscapes, from beachside resorts to hilltop venues and backwater locations. Choose a venue that aligns with your vision for the big day.
3. Local Traditions: Incorporate local customs and traditions into your wedding to make it more authentic and memorable.
4. Vendor Coordination: Engage local vendors who are familiar with the area and can provide you with the best services, including catering, decor, and entertainment.
5. Guest Accommodation: Ensure your guests are comfortably accommodated near the wedding venue. Kerala offers a range of options, from luxury resorts to cozy homestays.
6. Travel Arrangements: Plan for transportation to and from the venue for your guests, especially if the location is remote or requires travel by boat, which is common in backwater regions.
7. Permits and Legalities: Check for any necessary permits, especially if you're planning a beach or outdoor wedding, and ensure all legal requirements are met.
These tips will help you create a seamless and unforgettable destination wedding in the beautiful state of Kerala.

Which Part of Kerala is Best for a Destination Wedding?

In "God’s Own Country," Kerala offers an array of stunning locations where you can host your dream wedding and turn your fairy tale into reality. Here are some of the top spots to consider:

  • Alleppey: Renowned for its serene backwaters and spacious houseboats, Alleppey is a dream wedding destination. You can hold one or all of your wedding ceremonies on a houseboat, surrounded by natural beauty that will make your special day even more magical.
  • Kovalam: If a beach wedding is what you envision, Kovalam's pristine beaches are the perfect setting. The shimmering waters and golden sands provide a breathtaking backdrop for the beginning of your new life together.
  • Munnar: This picturesque hill station, nestled amidst lush greenery, majestic waterfalls, and cool mountain breezes, offers an incredibly romantic atmosphere. Munnar’s scenic landscapes provide the ideal backdrop for a wedding filled with unforgettable memories.

Best beaches for Destination Wedding in Kerala?

Getting married on a beach, feeling the sea breeze, and being surrounded by your loved ones is a dream for many. Here are some top beach destinations in Kerala for a perfect destination wedding:

  • The Leela Kovalam (Trivandrum) – Imagine starting your wedding day with breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea from a luxurious cliff-top venue. The Leela Kovalam offers stunning vistas, upscale accommodations, and a vibrant atmosphere, making it an ideal place to say ‘I do.’
  • Marari Beach Resort (Alleppey) – If you desire a blend of natural beauty and luxury, Marari Beach Resort is perfect. With its individual pools and charming thatched roof cottages, this resort provides a serene and authentic beach experience.
  • Turtle On the Beach (Thiruvananthapuram) – Combining a unique Japanese aesthetic with luxury, Turtle On the Beach offers a grand and distinctive setting for your wedding. Its luxurious amenities and distinctive design make it a standout choice for couples seeking a memorable celebration.

What are some of the best resorts to stay in Kerala for wedding ceremony?

Kerala’s natural beauty is celebrated by both locals and visitors, making it a dream destination for weddings. Here are some of the best resorts in Kerala for a wedding ceremony:

  • Kumarakom Lake Resort – This opulent five-star resort offers a royal experience with its stunning villas and picturesque surroundings. The spacious layout makes it an excellent choice for hosting a grand wedding ceremony.
  • Estuary Island Resort – Located on the shores of Poovar Island Beach, this four-star resort provides breathtaking views of both the beach and the backwaters. It’s an ideal setting for a destination wedding.
  • Ramada Resort – Overlooking the serene backwaters of Alleppey, this five-star resort is renowned for its modern infrastructure and facilities. Its beauty and elegance make it a perfect venue for your wedding celebration.

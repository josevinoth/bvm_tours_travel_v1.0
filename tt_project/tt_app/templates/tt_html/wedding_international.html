Tips for International Destination Weddings

Planning an international destination wedding can be both exciting and challenging. Here are a few tips to help ensure your wedding goes off without a hitch:

  • Plan Early: As soon as you finalize your wedding date, send out save-the-date notices to give your guests ample time to make travel arrangements. Consider organizing tastings early to finalize menu options for your invitations.
  • Distribute Travel Information Packages: Include a comprehensive travel package with your invitations about 5 months in advance. This should provide details on how to reach the destination and any visa requirements. Some visa officers may require a wedding invitation card, so early distribution is crucial.
  • Provide a Wedding Itinerary: Ensure your invitation and travel package include a detailed itinerary of the wedding events and timings. This will help guests plan their trip more efficiently. Additionally, consider including information on nearby tourist attractions and activities they can explore during their stay.
